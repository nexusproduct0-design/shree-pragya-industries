Our Story

Trusted Garlic Processor
from the Heart of MP

Shree Pragya Industries is one of India's trusted suppliers and manufacturers of premium peeled garlic products. Founded by Ashish Patidar, Deepak Patidar, and Vinay Patidar, we operate from Dalauda, Mandsaur — one of India's finest garlic-growing regions in Madhya Pradesh.

Our commitment to quality, hygiene, and innovative nitrogen packaging technology has made us a trusted partner for bulk buyers, hotels, restaurants, food processors, and wholesalers across India.

Our Process

From Farm to Your Doorstep

A rigorous 6-step process that guarantees every clove reaching you is clean, fresh, and perfectly packed.

Step 01
Farm Selection
Only premium garlic bulbs from carefully selected, certified farms in the Mandsaur region are procured.
🌾
🧹
Step 02
Cleaning & Sorting
Garlic bulbs are thoroughly washed, de-rooted, and sorted by size and quality grade before peeling.
Step 03
Hygienic Peeling
Food-grade mechanical peeling under sterile conditions — no chemicals, no contamination, just pure peeled cloves.
🧄
🔬
Step 04
Quality Check
Multi-parameter quality inspection: colour, size uniformity, aroma, and microbial testing on every batch.
Step 05
Nitrogen Packing
Approved cloves are sealed in nitrogen-flushed pouches — eliminating oxygen to preserve freshness and extend shelf life.
💨
🚚
Step 06
Fast Dispatch
Packed orders are dispatched same-day or next-day via temperature-controlled logistics to any Indian city.
